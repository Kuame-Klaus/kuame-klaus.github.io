Background

What I do

I'm a Senior Analyst at EY Ghana, working across Assurance and Data & Technology Risk. Most of my engagements involve testing and reviewing enterprise systems, banking cores, and ERPs for financial institutions across Ghana, Nigeria, Rwanda, Malawi, Tanzania, and Sierra Leone.

Outside client work, I spend a lot of time in financial modelling and equity research — building models for M&A scenarios, IPOs, and loan structures, mostly with a Ghanaian and West African context in mind.

I'm originally from Larteh, in the Akuapem hills, and it's a place I'd like to give something back to. One idea I keep returning to is founding a small cultural heritage foundation there — still early days, but it's a direction I'm actively exploring.
